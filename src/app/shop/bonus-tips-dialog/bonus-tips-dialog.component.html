How can I check my total bonus?
You can check your bonus history by following “Payment —> Balance —> Bonus Earned” on the navigation bar.
When can I withdraw my bonus?
Your monthly bonus will be paid 60 days from the end of the current month, because it takes time for us to deliver your orders and process potential refunds.
Does canceled or refunded orders affect my bonus?
Yes. For any canceled or refunded orders, it will be deducted from your sales revenue. If your adjusted revenue is lower than the original sales target you have reached, your bonus will also be adjusted accordingly.
How do I withdraw my bonus?
Your bonus will be calculated into your total earnings, so you can withdraw your bonus from your available balance. The money will be deposited into your selected payment method.
