Customer Support FAQs
How do I check on the status of my order?
Once your order is placed, you will get an order confirmation email including an “Guest Order Number”.
Click on View Your Order on the menu bar, and enter your Guest Order Number and email address to access your order information.
My order status is still under processing, when will it be shipped?
It takes between 1-5 business days for your items to be packed and shipped. You will receive an email notification from us when your order is shipped.
Can I change the shipping address after placing an order?
Yes, you can change your shipping address before the order has shipped. Please note that it’s no longer possible to change the address once your order has shipped.
Can I cancel my order after I place it?
If you want to cancel your order, please go to “View Your Order” section. For any items that haven’t been shipped, a “Cancel Order” button will appear. After clicking this button, your order will be canceled and no charges will occur.
How can I track my package?
If you want to check the delivery status of your package, you can find the tracking information by visiting “View Your Order” page. You will also receive a shipping notification email from us, in which contains your order tracking information.
Please note, for some free shipping items, there may not be tracking information available.
How much do you charge for shipping?
The shipping cost varies depending on the size, weight and destination of your order. We will strive to pick the best shipping methods for you.
Where does my order ship from?
We source products from brands and manufacturers from across the world, so your order could ship from a variety of locations depending on the suppliers.
What if I received a shipping email but the tracking link doesn’t work?
Emails get sent as soon as your package is shipped. Please allow 12-24 hours for the shipping carriers to update the tracking status in their system.
Will my package be charged custom duty and import charges?
In some cases, you may be charged for handling fees and taxes as your order passes through customs. Any charge on a package must be paid by the recipient. We have no control over these changes and cannot predict the amount of it, as customs and taxation policies vary widely from country to country.
What if my order arrives incomplete, damaged, or defective?
We offer full refund or free exchange for any incomplete, damaged or defective orders. If you encountered these issues, please go to “View Your Order” section right away where you can contact our customer support team.
What if my order has not arrived by the expected delivery date?
Sometimes there will be a few days’ delay for some orders, please do not worry if the tracking records are normal and updating.
What if my package say it’s been delivered but I don’t have it?
Once your package is marked as delivered by the shipping carrier, we’re no longer responsible for any lost or stolen goods. But if the parcel turns out missing, we will issue a full refund to you.
What is your return policy?
You may return any item within {{returnDays}} days of delivery. We will issue you a full refund or send you a new replacement once we receive your returned package. Please note that any returned items must be in an unused condition.
How do I return my package?
Step1: Initiate your return request under “View Your Order” section.
Step2: Customer service will send you a RAN (Return Authorization Number).
Step3: You pack up the item and ship it back to a specific warehouse address.
Step4: Once your package is returned, we will send you the refund or exchanged item within 3 business days.
Do you cover the shipping fee for item return?
Please note that any shipping fee of the returned package will not be included in the refund.
Where is my refund?
You can check the refund status by visiting “View Your Order” page and checking the specific order. Your refund will be sent to your original payment method. Please allow 3-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.
How to get help with my order?
For assistance, please go to “View Your Order” section where you can access your order information and contact our customer support. Please make sure to have Guest Order Number and email address.
