About Us
SocialCommer is a social commerce platform for youtubers and influencers to set up their online stores for free and earn money by promoting affiliate products of their favorites without the need for handling any inventory, shipping and customer queries.
As an affiliate store owner, you can build a customized and beautiful storefront to fit your personal style, list any products you wish to share with your friends and social media followers, and earn up to 10% commission when your audiences purchase any products in your store.
At SocialCommer, we believe in removing the barriers of running an ecommerce business and we want to make it possible for everyone to become a store owner of their favorite products and brands. Our vision is to build the world’s largest social commerce network powered by influencers and help brands and small businesses reach their customers globally.
As a Y Combinator-backed company, our team is comprised of industry experts and savvy-thought leaders in the digital industry including Forbes 30 Under 30, Y Combinator Alumni, Stanford Graduate, Ex-Google/Amazon employee, etc.
