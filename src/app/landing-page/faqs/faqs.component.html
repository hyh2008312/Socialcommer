SocialCommer FAQs
About Affiliate Online Store
What is Affiliate Online Store?
Affiliate Online Store is a new ecommerce model which allows people to easily set up their own online stores and make money by selling affiliate products of their choices without ever managing inventory, shipment and customer queries.
How is it different from other e-store such as Shopify?
To run an Affiliate Online Store, you don’t need to purchase any inventory, or handle product shipment and customer support by yourself. Everything will be done by the product supplier.
How do I earn money from my Affiliate Online Store?
First of all, apply for an affiliate program of a trusted ecommerce site such as Amazon, Aliexpress, etc.
Secondly, pick any products of your favorites and generate affiliate links from the ecommerce sites you’re affiliated with, and add them to your affiliate online store. Thirdly, keep promoting your store on your social media or any other channel, if there is a store visitor clicking on your product link and make a purchase, you will earn a sales commission from your affiliate ecommerce site.
What is needed to start an Affiliate Online Store?
Before running your affiliate online store, you need to apply to become an affiliate with at least one ecommerce site such as Amazon, eBay, Aliexpress, etc. This is required because you need to generate the affiliate links from those ecommerce sites and earn commissions for the products you promote.
Do I need to become an agent before selling a branded product in my store?
No, you don’t have to apply to become an agent for the branded product. But you need to apply to become an affiliate with an ecommerce site that has been selling the items you wish to promote in your store.
Do I need to register a business entity to run my Affiliate Online Store?
No, you don’t need to register a business entity. SocialCommer is open for any individual.
About SocialCommer
Is SocialCommer free?
SocialCommer currently only supports Starter Plan, which is 100% free.
Does SocialCommer take commission from my sales?
All the orders will be placed on the third-party marketplace you’re affiliated with, thus, SocialCommer doesn’t take any commissions out of your sales.
Is SocialCommer available in my country?
Yes! You can use SocialCommer to launch your affiliate online store from any country.
About Store Operation
Do I need to take care of inventory, shipment and customer support?
No, you don’t. The only thing you need to do is to keep promoting your online store.
Should I start with a Niche Store or a General Store?
One of the great things about SocialCommer is that you can sell practically everything, however, we do recommend that beginner should start with a niche store first. There are several advantages to starting out with a niche store: more focused, easier to find your target audience, spreading the word is also much simpler. Once you gain traction on your niche store, then you can expand your product categories towards a general store.
How many products should I list in my store?
The maximum number of products you have available in your store is totally up to you. But we recommend you to carefully pick 30-200 products to sell in your store, so your store visitor’s focus will not be scattered and they can explore what they’re interested in.
How can I track the total earnings of my Affiliate Online Store?
For all affiliate products imported from the third-party websites such as Amazon or Aliexpress, you can only track the sales volume by signing into your affiliate account in the third-party ecommerce sites.
Can I customize my storefront according to my preference?
Yes. We offer different templates for you to select for your storefront, meanwhile, you can also customize your storefront’s contents by defining your categories, products, banners and description in order to fit your brand and taste.
How to get my first sale?
We recommend leveraging your existing social media channels such as Youtube, Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to engage your followers and subscribers to visit your affiliate store. You can also share links to your store on relevant communities and forums, which your target audiences visit on a regular basis.
